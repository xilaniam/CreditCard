About Credit Cards

Credit cards are financial tools that offer users a convenient and flexible way to make purchases. These plastic cards, issued by banks and financial institutions, allow individuals to borrow money up to a predetermined limit. Users can make purchases, pay bills, and access credit, with the option to repay the borrowed amount over time. Credit cards often come with rewards, cashback offers, and various perks, making them a popular choice for daily transactions and building credit history.
